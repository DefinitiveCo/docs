## Order types

The most advanced order surface of any onchain trading API. Each type is one parameter change away.

| Type | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| **Market** | Execute immediately at the best available price. |
| **Limit** | Fill only at your specified price or better. |
| **Stop Loss** | Trigger a market sell when price falls to a threshold. |
| **Take Profit** | Trigger a market sell when price rises to a target. |
| **TWAP** | Break a large order into tranches over 10 min to 30 days, with privacy-preserving execution. |
| **DCA** | Recurring buys or sells at regular time intervals. |
| **Quick Trade** | Optimized with higher slippage settings for high-volatility or low-liquidity tokens. |

Most aggregators stop at Market. Flash is the only API that ships advanced order types across EVM and Solana.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Quote">
    Call `/quote` with your trade parameters. Flash returns the best route across 200+ liquidity sources and a signable payload.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign">
    The user signs the EIP-712 (EVM) or SPL (Solana) payload offchain.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Land">
    Submit the signed payload to `/order`, including the same `flashIntegratorFeeBps` value used for the quote. Flash handles gas, MEV protection, simulation, and onchain landing.
  </Step>
</Steps>
