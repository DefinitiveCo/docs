title: "Monetize Your Integration"
description: "Collect fees on every trade executed through Flash. Set your rate, attach your wallets, and start earning."

As an integrator, you can collect a fee on every trade Flash executes on behalf of your users. You choose the rate, Flash collects it at execution time, and the proceeds go directly to your Definitive vault.

## How it works

When you call Flash, include the `flashIntegratorFeeBps` parameter on both `POST /quote` and `POST /order`. Choose the fee before quoting, then submit the same string value with the signed order. Flash deducts your fee from the trade output and routes it to the Definitive vault controlled by your fee wallet.

| Concept | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| **Parameter** | `flashIntegratorFeeBps` |
| **Format** | String, in basis points. `"50"` = 0.50%, `"100"` = 1.00%. |
| **Where it goes** | Your Definitive vault, owned by the fee wallet connected to your integrator account. |
| **When to set it** | On `POST /quote` and `POST /order`, with the same value on both calls. |

## Set your fee on quote and order

Pass `flashIntegratorFeeBps` as a string when calling `POST /quote`. Example: buying WETH with 100 USDC on Base with a 50 bps fee.

```bash cURL
curl -X POST https://ddp.definitive.fi/v2/flash/quote \
  -H "x-definitive-api-key: YOUR_API_KEY" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "targetChain": "base",
    "contraChain": "base",
    "targetAsset": "0x4200000000000000000000000000000000000006",
    "contraAsset": "0x833589fCD6eDb6E08f4c7C32D4f71b54bdA02913",
    "side": "buy",
    "qty": "100",
    "orderType": "market",
    "flashIntegratorFeeBps": "50"
  }'
```

After the user signs the quote payload, submit the order with the same `flashIntegratorFeeBps` value.

```bash cURL
curl -X POST https://ddp.definitive.fi/v2/flash/order \
  -H "x-definitive-api-key: YOUR_API_KEY" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "targetChain": "base",
    "contraChain": "base",
    "targetAsset": "0x4200000000000000000000000000000000000006",
    "contraAsset": "0x833589fCD6eDb6E08f4c7C32D4f71b54bdA02913",
    "side": "buy",
    "qty": "100",
    "orderType": "market",
    "funderAddress": "0xUSER_WALLET",
    "quoteId": "q_abc123",
    "userSignature": "0x...",
    "evmOrderTypedData": "...",
    "flashIntegratorFeeBps": "50"
  }'
```

**To use the same fee on every trade**, hardcode `flashIntegratorFeeBps` in your integration logic and send it on both calls. **To vary fees per trade**, choose the value before `POST /quote` and reuse it unchanged on `POST /order`.

## Withdraw your fees

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Definitive">
    Go to [app.definitive.fi](https://app.definitive.fi) and sign in with your Flash integrator credentials.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect your fee wallet">
    Connect the EVM or Solana wallet associated with the vault you want to withdraw from.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Withdraw">
    Click **Withdraw** and sign the transaction in your wallet to move funds from your Definitive vault to your fee wallet.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Track your fees

Every fill returned by `GET /orders/{orderId}` includes fee fields so you can reconcile earnings programmatically.

| Fill field | What it contains |
| --- | --- |
| `feeAmount` | Total fee for this fill (in the fee token) |
| `feeTicker` | Token the fee was denominated in |
| `tradeFeeAmount` | Trade-related portion of the fee |
| `networkFeeAmount` | Network/gas portion of the fee |
| `feeNotional` | Total fee in notional (USD) terms |

## Quick reference

| Question | Answer |
| --- | --- |
| Where do I set my fee? | `flashIntegratorFeeBps` on `POST /quote` and `POST /order` |
| Can I change my fee per trade? | Yes. Set it on each order individually. |
| What if I omit the fee parameter? | No integrator fee is charged for that order. |
| Where do fees accumulate? | Your Definitive vault, controlled by your connected wallet. |
| How do I withdraw? | Sign in at [app.definitive.fi](https://app.definitive.fi), connect your wallet, click Withdraw. |
